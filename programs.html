Our Programs

BFF runs multiple programs to support youth development:

  • Scholarship Program We believe that financial barriers should never stand in the way of a bright future. Our Scholarship Program provides: Full and partial scholarships covering tuition, uniforms, textbooks, and stationery. Support for students at primary, secondary, and tertiary levels. Merit-based and needs-based awards to ensure that motivated learners have the opportunity to succeed.
  • Tutoring & Academic Support Our tutoring program strengthens the academic foundation of students by: Offering after-school classes in Mathematics, Science, English, and Life Skills. Providing one-on-one tutoring and group study sessions led by qualified educators and volunteers. Using digital learning tools to make lessons engaging and accessible. Tracking progress through regular assessments and feedback to parents/guardians.
  • Mentorship & Personal Development We pair students with dedicated mentors who guide them academically, emotionally, and socially. Our mentorship program includes: Career guidance to help students identify and work toward long-term goals. Life skills workshops on topics such as communication, teamwork, leadership, and financial literacy. Role model sessions with community leaders and professionals who inspire and motivate students. Career Development & Job Readiness Preparing students for life after school is central to our mission. We equip young people with practical tools to thrive in the workplace through: Resume writing and interview preparation workshops. Internships and learnership placements with local businesses. Career expos and networking events that connect students with potential employers. Entrepreneurship training, encouraging students to start small businesses and develop self-reliance.
  • Community Engagement & Leadership We believe in developing socially responsible leaders. This program fosters community involvement by: Encouraging students to participate in volunteer projects such as literacy drives, clean-up campaigns, and food distribution. Hosting youth leadership camps to build confidence, resilience, and teamwork. Creating opportunities for students to give back and inspire the next generation.

    All programs aim to create a sustainable impact on underprivileged communities.
