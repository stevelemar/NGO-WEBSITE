About Bright Future Foundation

Bright Future Foundation (BFF) was established in Johannesburg, South Africa in 2010. We empower underprivileged youth through education, mentoring, and skill development programs.

Mission

To provide education and mentorship support to disadvantaged youth in order to establish equitable opportunities for them.

Vision

A society in which all children have the means and abilities to realise their greatest potential.

Target Audience

Volunteers, sponsors, and low-income students between 10-25 years old.

Accomplishments

  • Over 5,000 students assisted.
  • Scholarships and tutoring programs provided.
  • Job development opportunities facilitated.
